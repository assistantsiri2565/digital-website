About Us

Maverick Software Technologies Pvt Ltd has
established itself as a premier industrial
automation training institute in India. We are
dedicated to bridging the gap between
theoretical knowledge and practical application

Robotics, PLC Training, and Engineering Design.
With years of experience, we have trained
thousands of engineers and placed them in
reputable organizations. Our expert trainers
ensure that you gain industry-relevant skills and
certifications. Whether you're a student or a
professional, our courses are designed to meet
the growing demands of the automation industry,
ensuring your career advancement.
